**Online Food Ordering System – Project Overview**

The **Online Food Ordering System** is a web-based application that allows users to browse food items, place orders, and view restaurant menus online. It provides a simple and user-friendly platform for customers to order food from the comfort of their homes.

### Objectives

* Provide an easy way to order food online.
* Display food items with prices and descriptions.
* Allow users to add items to a cart and place orders.
* Create a responsive design that works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

### Features

1. **Home Page** – Displays featured food items and offers.
2. **Menu Section** – Shows available food items with images and prices.
3. **Search Functionality** – Helps users find food items quickly.
4. **Cart System** – Add, remove, and update food quantities.
5. **User Profile** – Displays customer information.
6. **Order Summary** – Shows selected items and total bill.
7. **Responsive Design** – Works on all screen sizes.

### Technologies Used

* **HTML** – Structure of the website.
* **CSS** – Styling and responsive design.
* **JavaScript** – Interactive features like cart management.

### Benefits

* Saves time for customers.
* Easy navigation and ordering process.
* Accessible from any device with internet access.

### Conclusion

The Online Food Ordering System simplifies the food ordering process by providing a convenient, fast, and user-friendly platform. It enhances customer experience and demonstrates practical web development skills using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
